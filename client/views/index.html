How To Play

1

Each player begins with, and will always have, 10 white answer cards

2

For each round, one player is randomly chosen as the Card Czar

3

Everyone else answers the black question card by clicking on the answer card they want to use

4

Each player begins with, and will always have, 10 white answer cards

We Improve Human Lives

Cards for humanity strives to achieve a better state of life for all humans. To meet this goal, donations are used to provide people in slums and poverty prone areas with necessary resources to give them a better life. These resources include education, health care, food, clothing and anything that improves the lives of the people we are able to reach.
